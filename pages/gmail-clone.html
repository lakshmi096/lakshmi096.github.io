The demo gmail clone

A desktop version

Using this applications, you can login with your existing gmail account & upon successful authorization you will be redirect to a dashboard similar to gmail.

Featues present: Collapsible sidebar, Receive dummy emails with a button click with realtime update of mail list, Emails sorted into categories with distinction between read/unread mails, Detailed View of selected email, Ability to mark an email as starred or important, Navigation of filtered emails using sidebar as well top sections based on selection, Ability to compose & send a new mail with realtime update, Ability to save mail as a draft & be sent later, Ability to Logout of the application

Technologies used: HTML, CSS, React, Redux, Firebase, Firestore
