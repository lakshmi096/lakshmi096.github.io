The game tic-tac-toe

(Noughts and Crosses)

A classic Tic-Tac-Toe game (also called Noughts and Crosses), or Xs and Os/“X’y O’sies”, is a popular paper-and-pencil game for two players, X and O, who take turns marking the spaces in a 3×3 grid. The player who succeeds in placing three of their marks in a diagonal, horizontal, or vertical row is the winner.

Featues present: Two players can alternatively take chances & play their turn, declaring winner, ability to restart the game, user friendly toast messages.

Technologies used: HTML, CSS, React, Github Pages
